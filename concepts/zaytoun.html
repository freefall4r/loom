Our Philosophy

“We cook the way our grandmothers remembered —
only quieter.” — Est. in the old hills of Amman

Rooted in the soil of the Levant, plated for the present. Every dish begins at a single olive tree and ends in the warmth of an open flame.

Zaytoun is a study in patience. We ferment, we cure, we coax — letting time do the work that haste cannot. Our larder draws from terraced farms in Ajloun, sumac from the Galilee winds, and oil pressed within the same week it reaches your table. Nothing is loud. Everything is deliberate.

Khalil & Mariam Haddad Founders · Chef-Patron
