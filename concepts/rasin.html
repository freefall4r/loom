The Rasin way

From a single farm
to your kitchen counter.

We buy whole lots from growers we know by name — never a faceless blend. Every bag is traceable back to one farm, one altitude, one harvest.

Then we roast it gently, in batches small enough to taste twice a week. No rushing, no scorching. Just the bean, doing what it does best when you let it take its time.

— Yara & the Rasin roasting table
